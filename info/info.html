About:

MP is an online literary publication that publishes forward facing work. A piece is published when accepted. All prior published work is viewable in the Archive.

Begun in 2010, MP spent much of the following decade dormant. Now, the magazine is back online.

Submission Guidelines:

Paste story inside body of email. Attachments will be unopened and declined. Resubmissions of a previously declined work will not be considered. Once work has been submitted, please give two weeks before submitting another work.

Direct MP submissions here: mplitmag at gmail dot com
